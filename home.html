S E C T O R

A sector in a recruitment portal is a category that groups jobs by industry, function, or skill set. It helps recruiters organize and manage job postings more efficiently, while job seekers find relevant jobs based on their skills and interests. Common sectors include technology, finance, healthcare, education, sales, marketing, human resources, customer service, manufacturing, engineering, law, accounting, construction, and retail. For recruiters, selecting the sector that matches job requirements and adding keywords helps them find the opening. Job seekers can use sector filters and view profiles to learn about different industries.

P R A C T I C E

The practice section of a recruitment portal offers quizzes and practice questions to help users prepare for job interviews. These quizzes measure cognitive ability, verbal and numerical reasoning, personality, and interview question practice. The section also provides a list of common interview questions and a simulated interview environment. To use the practice section effectively, users should take a variety of quizzes, review their results, practice answering common interview questions in a simulated environment, and seek feedback from friends or family members. By following these tips, users can enhance their chances of success in job interviews.

R E V I E W

The review section in a recruitment portal allows candidates to share their experiences with the recruitment process, providing valuable insights for both candidates and employers. It helps identify areas for improvement, such as interview questions or interview formats. Writing a helpful review is important, with positive reviews helping others and negative ones helping employers.
